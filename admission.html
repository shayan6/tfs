Intermediate

The dates set for the admission in XI class are notified through the newspaper by the Director General Colleges Sindh.

Admission to pre-medical and pre-engineering classes are given to students who have taken science and technical groups in high school. For Cambridge students, admission will be granted only if a student has passed the Cambridge or G.C.E. 'O' level examination in at least 8 academic subjects including English, Islamiat and Pakistan studies.

Important

  • The Cambridge or G.C.E. 'O' level students are not eligible for provisional admission.
  • The subject is not allowed to candidates coming from other boards, if their marksheet do not show separate marks for theory and practical in Science subjects.
  • The subjects once offered in the admission form will not be changed.

Application for admission must be accompanied by:

  • Two passport size photographs of applicant.
  • Two envelopes with postage stamps
  • One photostate attested copy of provisional certificate
  • One photostate attested copy of C.N.I.C. for those above 18 years OR 'b' form.
  • Photostat attested copy of details marksheet
  • One photostat attested copy of Matric admit card.
  • Originals of all photo sets also required for verification.
